The London Eye:

The London Eye has been opened on December 31th 1999. This is why they also call it The Millennium Wheel. The structure is 135 metres tall and the wheel is in a diameter of 120 metres. When you want to enter the capsule to see the view of London, you have to enter it while it's still turning. I personally love the view from up there.

At the time of the construction, the London Eye was the tallest Ferris Wheel in the world. When you are at the highest point of the Wheel, you can look at the beautiful tower The Big Ben just near the Palace of Westminster.
